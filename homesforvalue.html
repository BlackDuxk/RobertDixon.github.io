Introduction

the value of a home

Selling a home isn’t easy and often results in depleted wallets and weeks, if not months, of uncertainty. Homes For Value is a real estate agency that makes selling a home less of a financial nightmare for home owners.

I turned one of the agency’s postcards into a website that helped increase their consumer base.

the Problem

opportunity left at the door

Homes For Value obtains new clients almost entirely through door to door sales. Every new client receives a postcard with the agency's information, however without a digital footprint the loss of a postcard means the loss of a client.

To overcome this challenge, I created a website for the company that captures the core values of the agency, and highlights their main selling points.

A key called accessibility

No amount of words or images can quite match the reassuring feeling home owners get talking to a real person about the value of something that matters to them.

To match this feeling as closely as possible, the website features visuals that shares characteristic to the homes of past clients, and wording that reaffirms the core message told by every agent.

Aligning Experiences

As we know, selling a home is no easy task, so it’s no surprise if a small, thin, paper postcard gets lost in all the chaos.

For that reason the Homes For Value website has a responsive design. This way home owners can never lose access to information vital to their selling experience, and can even share the information with other homeowners in similar situations.

Insight

The Value of emotion

Aligning the experience home owners have on a website to the experience they have talking to another human being is an entirely different beast compared to making information more accessible to homeowners.

Learning how to dissect the emotional elements in the conversations had with home owners, and recreating them digitally was the biggest factor that contributed to the growth of Homes For Value.
