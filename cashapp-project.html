Cash App

Putting People
Before
Payments

4 weeks

40 screens

The problem

Payments shouldn’t be stressful

Sending someone money is an already nerve wracking process, having to worry about making mistakes makes it even more stressful. Cash App offers an easy and instant means to complete transactions, however users hesitate when it’s time to hit pay.

This redesign addresses the reasons users hesitate and removes any additional unnecessary stress from an already stressful process.

Reassurance at a glance

Instead of seeing an inaccurate cash balance, users can quickly gain insight into their finances to decide whether they need to add additional funds before beginning a transaction.

A little reminder

By reminding users about the decisions they made during a transaction, users can quickly catch when $10 accidentally turns into $100.

Memorization made easy

The Favorites feature allows users to use preset data about past transactions to complete new ones, reducing the amount of time and steps needed to complete a transaction.

Insight

The undeniable truth

By introducing solutions into Cash App’s payment process focused on the likelihood of users making mistakes users can start and complete transactions with confidence in the choices they’ve made.

The less users have to memorize, the less stressful sending money to someone can be.
