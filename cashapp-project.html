Cash App

Matching The Reviews
With The Ranking

An exploration of how to improve Cash App’s core features.

Problem

Ranking vs. Reviews

Despite being the number one ranked finance app on Apple’s App Store, Cash App has received numerous one to two-star reviews from users commenting that they would rather use other products, like Venmo or PayPal, for their financial needs.

Research

A One Star Review

Cash App allows users to spend, send, save, and invest their money without the use of a bank account. However, research shows that relevant information is not accessible to users, leading them to question their decisions, while using the app’s core features.

These features need to be redesigned to match the experience users have using competing products, and to include related information in areas where users feel uncertain.

Design

Solution

Account Balance

A user’s account balance is accurately displayed on the app’s homepage so that users can start a transaction without wondering how much they have in their account.

Account Details

The ability to favorite another person’s account assures users that, no matter how many different accounts a person has, they are sending money to the right one.

Icons legitimizing another person’s account instills trust in users so that they don’t have to worry about being scammed when sending money to someone new.

Payment Verification Screen

A payment verification screen gives users a chance to check if they’ve made any mistakes before finalizing a transaction.

Accessible Information

Tabs allowing users to switch between different types of transactions help them distinguish between money they spent and money they received.

While detailed information about a transactions are immediately viewable, allowing users to stay informed their payment history.

Recurring Payments

Users now have the ability to preset and presave payments, bypassing the original payment process and all the potenial mistakes that come with it.

Bitcoins and Stocks

New and experienced investors can now find answers to their most frequently asked questions using newly added informative guides on the Bitcoin and Stocks page.
