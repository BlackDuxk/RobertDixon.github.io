About Me

I am a designer based in Maryland that operates at the point where brand and audience meet.

I talk to brands to understand both their product and the desired relationship they want with their audience. I then sit down with users to discuss what they need and want from that product. Then I create practical solutions aimed to address the wants and needs of each party, and that enriches the relationship between brand and audience.
