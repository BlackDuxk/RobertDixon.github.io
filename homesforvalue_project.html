Homes For Value

The Problem

Opportunity Left At The Door

Homes For Value is a real estate agency that makes selling your home less of a nightmare, however with the steady downfall of door-to-door sales and the reliability of print marketing the company's clientele began to shrink.

To overcome this challenge, I added some technology to their marketing method to increase cusotmer impact and growth.

Solutions

Aligning Experiences

Instead of homeowners losing that sense of connection the second they say farewell to agents, the moment they look at the company’s website they are reminded of their conversation through the use of words and images that reaffirm the company's core messaging.

Accessibility Is The Key

By making the company’s website responsive, the loss of a paper pamphlet doesn’t result in the loss of a customer. Instead, home owners can access and even share vital information that can save them from the nightmare that is selling a home.

Insight

The Value Of Emotion

Reproducing the emotional element in the conversation between homeowner and agent at the door in the company’s website helped Homes For Value not only grow their clientele but build a lasting relationship with them.
