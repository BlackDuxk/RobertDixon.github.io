Homes For Value

The Problem

Time For A Change

Recognizing the declining reliability of using print marketing to generate new business, Homes For Value wanted to transform a postcard, left with potential clients, into a website clients can use to easily reconnect with the agency.

Solutions

Using Accessibility As The Key

Instead of having the loss of a piece of paper resulting in the loss of a client, agents at Homes For Value can leave clients with a loss-proof, digital means to reconnect with the agency that is accessible as long as they have access to a device with internet.

Aligning Experiences

Homes For Value’s website uses choice text and visuals that are consistent with the conversational script and core messaging of the agency to invoke the same sense of trust and security clients have during their initial conversation with an agent.

Insight

The Value Of Emotion

By replacing a postcard with an accessible, digital means to reconnect that is reminiscent of a reassuring conversation between a homeowner and an agent, Homes For Value is able to rely on a more reliable marketing method to not only grow their clientele but build a lasting relationship with them.
