The Problem

The Illusion Of Inexperience

MirandaHolliday.com is Miranda’s online introduction to both Talent and Literary Agencies however, as a relatively new actress and playwright her work in each area is limited.

To combat this limitation, I elevated her existing work to create a first impression that communicated a sense of professionalism rather than inexperience.

Solutions

Ditching The Template

Instead of following a template, Miranda's acting portfolio is designed to reflect the preferences of its target audience. This takes away unnecessary distractions allowing her work to dominate the screen as well as the users attention.

Attention To Detail

Miranda’s writing portfolio uses limited but choice wording paired with vibrant visuals to arouse the user's senses. This pairing produces a gripping atmosphere that excites users and raises their engagement with displayed content.

Insight

Quality Over Quantity

Combining a minimalistic approach with a cinematic mindset produced a solution that allowed users to look beyond the illusion of inexperience and towards the brilliant talent within a young professional's work.
