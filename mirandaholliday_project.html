Introduction

the woman behindthe mask

MirandaHolliday.com is the website of a talented actress and a brillant playwright. The website serves as Miranda’s online persona to Talent and Literary Agencies, and highlighs both her work and personality.

I turned Miranda’s vision into a professional brand identity that not only sets her apart from her peers, but acts as a resource she can capitilize on.

The Problem

the illusion of inexperience

Since Miranda is relatively new as both a professional actress and playwright her work in both areas is greatly limited. This limitation creates an illusion of inexperience despite her talent.

To combat this, I elevated her work using unique designs to give it that sense of professionalism Talent and Literary Agencies look for.

Ditching the template

Cookie cutter templates commonly used by aspiring talents don’t impact and charm Talent Agencies like something personal and original.

The primary element used in Miranda’s acting portfolio is her content. Allowing her content to dominate the screen, unimpeded by distraction, makes users feel like they’re watching the trailer for a blockbuster film; eyes glued to the screen.

like watching a movie

Insight

Quality over Quantity

The best way to overcome the shortcoming of a lack of content is to capitalize on existing content.

Shifting my mindset to that of, “quality over quantity” allowed me to use a minimalistic design to help the most important part of Miranda’s portfolio, her personality and talent, dominate the viewer’s experience. This permitted her existing content to shine that much brighter, rather then other design elements.
