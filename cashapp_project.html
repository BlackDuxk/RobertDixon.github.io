Introduction

Banking with less paperwork

Cash App is a free mobile payment service that allows users to send, spend, save, and invest their money without the use of a bank account.

I improved the app’s payment process to reduce the anxiety user felt while completing transactions.

The Problem

payments shouldn’t be stressful

Do I have enough money in my account? Did I type in the right number? This is their cash tag, right?

Second guessing every decision they make during a transaction leaves users mentally exhausted, and anxious to find less taxing means to complete a payment.

To keep users using Cash App the app’s been redesigned to help users remain confident as they navigate through the payment process.

Information at a Glance

Cash App is a free mobile payment service that allows users to send, spend, save, and invest their money without the use of a bank account.

I improved the app’s payment process to reduce the anxiety user felt while completing transactions.

Less mistakes more money

Reminding users about the decisions they made while completing a payment eliminates their urge to second guess themselves during and at the end of a payment.

Instead of feeling anxious while a payment is being processed, users can remain confident in their decisions, in the fact, they would’ve easily noticed any mistake made.

Memorization made easy

The Favorites feature allows users to use saved data about past transactions to complete new ones.

Instead of having to constantly verify information, like a person’s cash tag, during a payment users can use preset data to complete payments instantly and with certainty.

Insight

The thing about stress

Whether it’s typing in the wrong number or sending money to the wrong person when it comes to money, users are afraid of making mistakes.

I can’t stop users from making mistakes however I can help them quickly notice and correct mistakes as they make them. Which results in less calls to customer service and a more enjoyable payment experience for users.
